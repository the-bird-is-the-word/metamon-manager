About

In November 2021, I got into the Radio Caca project. Few months later, I became aware that I need a tool that plays automatically for me on Metamon Island and that gathers simple statistics such as the generated egg fragments. In the following weeks, Metamon Manager was born.

The features of the website are summarized as follows:

  • Your Metamons can play automatically on Metamon Island via the Browser. You can choose how to decide on an opponent, i.e., whether to choose the weakeast opponent or a random one. If you wish so, Potions (for an N Metamon) or Diamonds (for R, SR, and SSR Metamons) are applied automatically to level up the Metamon. Eggs are minted after the battles.
  • Statistics on your battles (such as win-percentage and won egg fragments) are collected. They are presented in a table for each Metamon.
