Privacy Notice

This privacy policy will explain how Metamon Manager uses the personal data that is collected when you use the website. It is intended to comply with the GDPR laws of the EU.

Which data is collected and why?

Data on your Metamons (ID, rarity, power, metamon level, experience, league level) and battles (date and time, wallet, metamon id, number of battles, number of battles won, won egg fragments, league level, metamon level) are saved to provide the users statistics. If you don't want this data to be collected, uncheck the field "Collect statistics" and this data is not saved!

Metamon Manager collects data about the visits to the website in server log files. They are intended to monitor the functioning of the website. This data is (and cannot be) assigned to a specific person. The data consists of:

  • Date and time of request
  • URL of request
  • Used browser and operating system
  • IP address of the user

If you contact me via email, it is stored to resolve your specific issue.

To monitor the number of users of the website and their behaviour, I use Google Analytics. It is intended to improve the services on the website. Google Analytics uses cookies. Information about the usage of the website are collected and reported by Google. When using Google Analytics, IP addresses of the users are anonymized. Google is certified under Privacy Shield.

To monetize some of the traffic to the website, A Ads is used. They set cookies to analyze returning visitors to the website.

How is your data stored and when is it deleted?

The website stores your data securely at a data center of Linode in Tokyo, Japan. The newest Transport Layer Security (TLS) cryptographic protocols are used to transfer your data securely and to restrict access to your data.

Metamon Manager will keep the server logs and emails for 3 months. Once this time period has expired, we will delete your data. If you would like to have your (non-personal) game data deleted, please write me an email.

What are your data protection rights?

Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request for copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request to complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, I have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact me via email.
